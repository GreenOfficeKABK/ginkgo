The Ginkgo ants knew sustainability wasn’t just an ant issue — it was something the whole forest needed. So they began working with bees, beetles, butterflies, and all kinds of creatures, sharing knowledge and building together. That spirit of unity lives on in our Big Collaboration section. Welcome to the Ginkgo Colony — A Story of Sustainability and Community

Deep in the heart of the forest lived a remarkable ant colony called Ginkgo. Unlike others that focused only on gathering food, the Ginkgo ants had a bigger mission: to build a thriving, sustainable community where every ant could learn, share, and help care for their home. Just as the colony lived in harmony with nature, our website does too — powered entirely by solar energy. It’s a small but meaningful step toward a greener future, showing that technology and sustainability can grow hand in hand.
